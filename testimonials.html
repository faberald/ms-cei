Testimonials

The course is neatly structured for us to start with the ideation stage (Design and Innovation), move to customer discovery (Lean Launchpad), build a prototype (Foundations of Mobile Networks) and ultimately bring it all together and build the product (Entrepreneurship Capstone). This lets us go through the entire lifecycle of a startup inside a cocoon and make mistakes as fast as we can.

Anuj Menta, Class of 2020

The MS-CEI program offers an opportunity for students who want to develop stronger business and management skills than normal computer science graduates. It also provides a series of computer science courses taught by world-class faculty like Big Data, Computer Vision, and Deep Learning. The curriculum of this program is designed to facilitate the process of building your startup and make you become a successful tech leader.

Hongtao Liu, Class of 2020

As someone without a background in tech but with managerial experience, I felt a pure MS-CS would be too technical for me, and an MBA lacked the technical know-how I was searching for. The MS-CEI program allowed me to get perfect balance of business, entrepreneurial and technical skills I needed to pivot my career towards the tech industry. Beyond that, my team and I are taking full advantage of the program and NYU’s incredible resources to launch our own Startup!

Manuel Lombardero Botey, Class of 2020

The MS-CEI offers a unique experience bridging business courses with Tech MBAs at Stern and computer science courses with MS CS students at Courant. This accelerated one year masters program is equal parts challenging and rewarding, and results in the creation of well-balanced business and technology leaders who are raring to boldly create.

Nivedita Ganesh, Class of 2020


Being in early stage startups throughout my professional life, I was looking for a way to see the other side of the table through the lens of a founder. From my experience of trying to start a business in my undergrad program, I was aware that I needed a strong network and personal skills to launch a successful business. The bouquet of courses offered by the MS-CEI program across business, product and tech along with the strong focus on entrepreneurship was the perfect match for me.

Projjol Banerji, Class of 2020

MS-CEI has the right balance of courses at Courant and Stern which helps to improve business as well as technical aspects that are required for building a startup. The course facilitates you to build a startup by providing you all the tools that are needed.

Rishabh Yadav, Class of 2020



I really like that the MS-CEI program has a balance between between business courses at Stern and computer science courses at Courant. Additionally, I have enjoyed getting to know people from different backgrounds.

Yuexi Ding, Class of 2019

CEI is not a traditional MS CS program and that’s the best part. The whole experience revolves around solving real world problems and taking the relevant academic learning that helps you with your product.

Yash Goel, Class of 2019


The MS-CEI program offers a great mix of key business and CS classes for technical startup founders. Working hands-on with NYU professors, we have developed and implemented our customer discovery process and brought our product out of the lab and into the market.

Michael Gold, Cass of 2019

The MS-CEI program motivates you to become an entrepreneur. Combining Courant graduate computer science courses with Stern business school, you can get the right tools to lead in the industry.

Omer Katz, Class of 2019


MS-CEI program is the best program I have found to do your own startup, especially for international students. During this program, you will take rigorous Computer Science and Entrepreneurship courses and be exposed to a full cycle of building a startup.

Yonghao Liu, Class of 2019

I choose this program because it aims to cultivate student entrepreneurship and help students turn ideas into business realities. This program has both MBA courses and computer science courses which match my expectations well, and it has already helped me to advance through the early stages of tech entrepreneurship.

Eagle Tong, Class of 2019


My experience at NYU, and particularly in the MS-CEI program has been amazing! The program was both challenging and supportive. Through this experience, I not only obtained knowledge in entrepreneurship methodology, but also was provided with best opportunities to expand my skill-set to build a startup.

Doyeon Kong, Class of 2019

On the business side of stating a startup, the MS-CEI program has given us a chance to learn essential skills in business, leadership, and entrepreneurship at the Stern School of Business. I had a background in engineering with little to no experience in business and these Stern courses, taken over the summer, were not only beneficial and applicable to my startup, but it has also taught me skills which will be useful later on. From learning how to be a great leader in the Leadership class to planning out a course of actions to occupy the market in the Strategy class. Overall, this program is the perfect blend for anyone looking to start a startup, whether you are new to this or you are an experienced entrepreneur.

Sakada Lim, Class of 2019
